About NoteByNote

NoteByNote is a music tutorial tool built for anyone who wants to learn to play their favourite songs. Search a song, see the chords displayed above the exact lyric where each chord change happens, and follow along with the embedded video.

Built with React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB as a collaborative student project.
